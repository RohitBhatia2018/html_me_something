Rohit's 2021 Adventure Journal

I love going on adventures.
I also love sharing my adventures with others.
This is a space where I reflect on my experiences so we can relive them together!

Adventure #1: Surfing in Costa Rica

I spent a week surfing in Tamarindo, Costa Rica.
This was my first solo trip.
Getting outside of my comfort zone and meeting new people was a great experience.
I also learned that surfing is really hard, but I did catch a couple big waves!

Adventure #2: Skiing in Colorado

I spent 8 days skiing at Sunlight Mountain in Colorado with my friend Meg and her family / friends.
The highlights were getting a lot better at navigating moguls, learning a super fun card game called Squeak, and meeting some awesome people.
